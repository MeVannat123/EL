About El-FastFood

Fresh Coffee • Delicious Pastries • Cozy Vibes

Welcome to El-FastFood

El-FastFood is a modern coffee & bakery shop established in 2024 with one simple mission — to bring people together through the love of coffee, pastries, and warm smiles.

We carefully select high-quality beans, bake our pastries fresh daily, and create an atmosphere where everyone feels at home. Whether you’re here for a quick espresso or to enjoy time with friends, El-FastFood is your perfect destination.

Our Story

It all started with a small dream — to create a space where people could enjoy both great coffee and great company. From a small corner shop, El-FastFood has grown into a beloved café known for its quality drinks, freshly baked goods, and friendly service.

Our Mission

To serve freshness, quality, and happiness in every cup and bite.

Our Vision

To become the most loved coffee & bakery brand in Cambodia and beyond.
